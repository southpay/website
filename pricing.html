Pricing questions

Honest answers about the numbers.

If the pricing on this page raises a question, here's where we answer it.

Talk to a founder
Are there any setup fees, monthly minimums, or annual contracts? +
No. None of the three products have setup fees, monthly minimums, or annual contracts. Commerce and Invoice charge a flat % per transaction. Ledger is month-to-month and cancellable from the dashboard.
Do I need to commit to a tier? +
No. Commerce and Invoice tier descriptions are just for clarity - there's no separate signup. Ledger lets you pick a tier in the dashboard and change it whenever your usage changes.
What does the 1.99% on Commerce and Invoice actually include? +
Everything we charge for. No PCI compliance fees, no interchange, no gateway fees, no chargeback insurance, no FX surcharge for cross-border transactions. The number you see at checkout is the number we keep.
When do I pay on Commerce and Invoice? +
Only when you get paid. The fee is deducted from each transaction at settlement. If a customer never pays, neither do you.
What counts as a "posting" on Ledger? +
A posting is one debit or credit on one account. A typical payment transaction has 2 to 4 postings (user debit, merchant credit, platform fee credit, etc). We meter postings, not API calls, so a single balanced transaction with three legs counts as three postings.
When do I qualify for Custom pricing? +
We offer Custom pricing on Commerce and Invoice for high-volume merchants, marketplaces, and platforms. There's no fixed threshold. If you're processing meaningful volume each month and want a better rate, book a call and we'll work out a deal that fits your business.
When should I talk to you about Enterprise? +
When you need custom pricing, a dedicated environment, white-label, a contract uptime SLA, or compliance support beyond the standard tiers. If self-serve covers it, there's no reason to call.
