What verticals do you actually serve? +
Peptides and research chemicals, nutraceuticals and SARMs, CBD/hemp/kratom, firearms/ammo/tactical. If you're borderline or in another restricted category, email us. We take the conversation before we say no.
How is this different from Commerce? +
Commerce is our self-serve crypto checkout at 1.99% flat. Customers pay in crypto, you settle in USD. Card2Crypto keeps the customer experience as a card payment: they swipe a Visa, we handle the acceptance and settlement on a rail built for your vertical. Different product, different rail, different risk model.
Why doesn't this page show a price? +
Because the number depends on your vertical, monthly volume, average ticket, refund rate, and where funds land. A one-size-fits-all rate would either overcharge low-risk merchants or bankrupt us on the higher-risk ones. We quote in the intro call.
Are there monthly fees or setup fees? +
No monthly subscription. No setup fee. Per-transaction pricing only, quoted per merchant.
Do you hold rolling reserves? +
No standing reserve as a default. For very high refund or chargeback verticals we may agree a short-term reserve during onboarding, but the "hold 15% for six months just because" model does not apply here.
Where do funds settle? +
To your US bank account in USD, typically same-day or next-day depending on volume and vertical. Prefer stablecoin? We support USDT/USDC settlement on request.
Are chargebacks really impossible? +
On the crypto leg of settlement to you, yes. It's final and irreversible. On the acceptance leg from the customer's card, standard card-network dispute rules apply upstream on our rail. We absorb that risk as part of the service, not you.
How long does onboarding take? +
Usually inside two weeks from first email to first processed charge. Underwriting review, contract, integration walk-through, then live.

Reliable card payment processing for high-risk merchants

Card2Crypto is SouthPay's high-risk merchant account alternative, purpose-built for verticals that traditional payment processors (Stripe, Square, PayPal, Adyen, Braintree) either refuse outright or terminate mid-contract. We combine card acceptance with crypto-rail settlement so that regulated but PSP-restricted categories can operate a compliant, reliable checkout without the freezes, reserves, and MATCH-list risk that come with mainstream processors.

Categories we specialize in:

  • Peptide payment processing: BPC-157, TB-500, GHK-Cu, HGH fragments, research peptides shipped for research use only.
  • Nutraceutical and SARM merchant accounts: RAD-140, MK-677, LGD-4033, YK-11, plus general supplements, nootropics, and hormone-support stacks.
  • CBD, hemp, kratom, and delta merchant services: full-spectrum CBD, delta-8 THC, delta-9 hemp-derived, kratom powder and extracts, and adjacent botanicals compliant with the 2018 Farm Bill.
  • Firearms and FFL payment processing: 2A retailers, ammunition sellers, tactical gear, optics, and firearms training services.

If you're searching for a reliable card processor for peptides, a CBD merchant account that won't freeze, a SARM payment gateway, or a firearms-friendly payment processor, that's what Card2Crypto is. We're not a general PSP that "makes exceptions". This is our reason for existing.
