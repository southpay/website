The problem

You already know the story.

You didn't come to this page because your payments are working. You came because the account you spent months onboarding just froze, or your reserve is choking cash flow, or your third processor this year hit the MATCH list.

Account terminations without notice.

You wake up to a 30-day off-boarding email and 90 days of held funds. No hearing, no appeal. The MCC changed at your acquiring bank and you were the collateral.

Rolling reserves that eat your runway.

10 to 20% of monthly volume held for six months. On a business doing $500K/mo, that's $100K locked up at any time. That's your Q1 marketing budget sitting on someone else's balance sheet.

Chargebacks stacked against you.

Regulated verticals get treated as fraud-prone by default. Every dispute goes to the customer, plus $25 in fees. Card2Crypto settlements are final on the crypto side — no reversals downstream.

MCC blacklists you'll never see.

Stripe and its peers publish a "prohibited businesses" list that reads like a wishlist. What you don't see: the internal MCC codes acquirers use to shut off categories mid-quarter. You get to be the incident.

Reliable card payment processing for high-risk merchants

Card2Crypto is SouthPay's high-risk merchant account alternative, purpose-built for verticals that traditional payment processors — Stripe, Square, PayPal, Adyen, Braintree — either refuse outright or terminate mid-contract. We combine card acceptance with crypto-rail settlement so that regulated but PSP-restricted categories can operate a compliant, reliable checkout without the freezes, reserves, and MATCH-list risk that come with mainstream processors.

Categories we specialize in:

  • Peptide payment processing — BPC-157, TB-500, GHK-Cu, HGH fragments, research peptides shipped for research use only.
  • Nutraceutical and SARM merchant accounts — RAD-140, MK-677, LGD-4033, YK-11, plus general supplements, nootropics, and hormone-support stacks.
  • CBD, hemp, kratom, and delta merchant services — full-spectrum CBD, delta-8 THC, delta-9 hemp-derived, kratom powder and extracts, and adjacent botanicals compliant with the 2018 Farm Bill.
  • Firearms and FFL payment processing — 2A retailers, ammunition sellers, tactical gear, optics, suppressors (where legal), and firearms training services.

If you're searching for a reliable card processor for peptides, a CBD merchant account that won't freeze, a SARM payment gateway, or a firearms-friendly payment processor — that's what Card2Crypto is. We're not a general PSP that "makes exceptions". This is our whole reason for existing.
