SouthPay Builders The team behind the product

Building the bridge between crypto buyers and ecommerce stores.

SouthPay is a global crypto payment solution built for the way commerce actually works today. Accept USDT, USDC, and 60+ cryptocurrencies with one simple integration - settle in cash, stablecoins, or crypto.

Our mission

Make crypto a checkout option, not a project.

Crypto is no longer optional. Stores that accept crypto convert more customers, pay less in fees, and expand globally, instantly.

But every existing path to "accept crypto" comes with months of integration work, KYC trees that scare off merchants, and settlement flows that leave you holding a coin you didn't want.

SouthPay makes that transition effortless. One integration. Flat 1.99%. Zero chargebacks. Settle however you want - USD wire, stablecoin balance, or hold the crypto.

What we believe

Three principles we don't compromise on.

01

The complexity stays on our side.

Wallets, networks, volatility, settlement. Merchants shouldn't have to learn any of it. They should see "+ $260.00, USD" and move on.

02

Pricing is transparent or it's broken.

No interchange-plus games. No surprise FX spreads. 1.99% flat is the number you pay - that's the number we charge.

03

The store owner is the customer.

Every decision goes through a single filter: does this make a busy merchant's day easier? If not, it doesn't ship.
