2. Definitions and interpretation

For purposes of this Agreement, the following definitions apply:

Platform
means the proprietary software platform, plugins, APIs, integrations, dashboards, administrative tools, configuration layers, and related technical infrastructure operated by ALC Media under the SouthPay brand.
Third-Party Payment Service Providers / PSPs
means independent third-party entities that provide blockchain transaction execution, payment processing, settlement, fiat conversion, custody (if any), compliance services, or related financial infrastructure.
Crypto-to-Crypto Track
means transactions in which end users pay in cryptocurrency and the Merchant is settled in cryptocurrency, without fiat conversion.
Crypto-to-Fiat Track
means transactions in which end users pay in cryptocurrency and the Merchant is settled in fiat currency through a PSP.
KYB
means know-your-business, business verification, corporate identity verification, ultimate beneficial owner identification, anti-money laundering (AML), counter-terrorist financing (CTF), sanctions screening, and related compliance procedures required by applicable law or by a PSP in connection with Merchant onboarding or continued service.

  • All payment execution, settlement, conversion, custody (if any), compliance checks, and related financial services are performed exclusively by Third-Party Payment Service Providers.

ALC Media does not perform identity verification or KYC of Merchant's end users. Any end-user compliance procedures are conducted solely by applicable PSPs, if required.

Merchant further acknowledges that ALC Media has no control over, and no responsibility for, the internal policies, technical systems, compliance determinations, or operational decisions of any PSP.

4.1 Crypto-to-Crypto Track

Where Merchant participates in the Crypto-to-Crypto Track:

  • Settlement is made exclusively in cryptocurrency.
  • KYB may not be required by ALC Media for participation in this track, subject to PSP requirements and applicable law.
  • Any compliance obligations are determined solely by the applicable PSP and/or applicable law.

Merchant bears all risks related to:

  • cryptocurrency price volatility

ALC Media shall have no liability for losses arising from any of the foregoing.

4.2 Crypto-to-Fiat Track

Where Merchant participates in the Crypto-to-Fiat Track:

  • Merchant acknowledges that KYB is mandatory for participation and continued access to settlement services.
  • Merchant agrees to provide accurate, complete, and up-to-date corporate documentation, ownership information, director information, and any other documentation reasonably required for KYB verification.
  • Merchant acknowledges that failure to satisfy KYB requirements may result in delayed settlement, rejected transactions, frozen funds, withheld balances, or permanent suspension by PSPs or authorities.

ALC Media has no responsibility for, and no ability to override, PSP or regulatory decisions.
